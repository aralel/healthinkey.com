Privacy and control

Health data is sensitive. The landing page reflects a privacy-first approach: you choose what to store and what to share.

  • Sharing is intentional (create a pack, then share it)
  • Clear access boundaries for family vs. doctors
  • Security messaging without overpromising

FAQ

Is this for me or for my family?

Both. Keep your own records and share selected info with relatives when needed.

Can I share my records with a doctor for a second opinion?

Yes. The idea is to create a clean “share pack” so a doctor has the right context remotely.

Is there an app already?

This repo is currently the landing website. Product features should be linked/added as they ship.
